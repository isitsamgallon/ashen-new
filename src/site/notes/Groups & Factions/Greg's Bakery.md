### Overview 
A recently opened bakery on the main street of [[Locations/OFC/Blackwall/Blackwall\|Blackwall]] inspired by the real-world British bakery and fast food chain, [Greggs Bakery](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Greggs).

### Notable Information
They are perhaps best known for their signature product Prophetic 'Bread' a type of bread that included a fortune stamped on the bottom of the slice that had a habit of coming true in various mundane or silly ways. 

### Known Fortunes
- The rain is wet. 
- Watch out for the third spoon
- Beware of Dories
