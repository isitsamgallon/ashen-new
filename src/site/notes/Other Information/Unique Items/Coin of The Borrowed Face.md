**Item (Wondrous Item) | Rare | No Requirement** 
A silver coin with no fixed face. it's image changes depending who is looking at it
_____________
------

**Charges**: 1
**Properties**: Coin,Magical
**Recharge**: Regains 1 charge on a Long Rest
**Value**:  12,000 - 18,000 gp 

----

### Features
Coin of Many Faces: once per Long Rest, the user can flip this coin and speak the name of a humanoid you have seen with the last 24 hours. For the next 10 minutes, your face, voice and general appearance shift to resemble that person. This function is similar to [[Admin/Spells/Disguise Self\|Disguise Self]], but only for that specific person. 

A creature can see through the coin's affects upon succeeding a **DC15 Intelligence (Investigation)** check.  

### Drawbacks & Curse
This coin sometimes also copies the small mannerisms of the copied person, even if the user did not intent to imitate them.
