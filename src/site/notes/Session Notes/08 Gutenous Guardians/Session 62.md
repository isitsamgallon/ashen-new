# Session Summary 
After a successful but exhausting raid, the Fatebreakers returned to Blackwall to find their new military commander, Kase, waiting with impeccable formality. While Halfbie privately revealed to Steve and Keith that the party had actually secured a staggering million gold—not the modest sum they'd claimed, the group debriefed and learned that the three "New God" children (Seer, Myst, and newly hatched Cas) had been left in their care. Viktor gifted Mordecai his magical ring, and the party decided their next destination should be the desert to seek out Shoni for answers about the gods, Aidan, and possibly help for the children. However, a scrying attempt on their former ally Empress Obeus revealed her brutally executing rebels, including Lord Enelion, suggesting she had become a ruthless tyrant.

That night brought disturbing developments: Halfbie woke to find their room covered in advanced magical formulae they had no memory of writing, which Viktor suspected might be the work of the entity sharing their body. Meanwhile, Drache dreamed of the Korrigan in the fading Feywild, reluctantly agreeing to a pact that would allow the fey to visit his dreams in exchange for causing future chaos. In the morning, Mordecai spent time with Cas, who matter-of-factly acknowledged she was dying from having too much magical power. Rather than pushing her toward a dangerous "fix," Mordecai promised to be her friend forever and accepted her exactly as she was, prompting Cas to declare herself "bringer of perfect." The session ended with the party preparing to leave for the desert while managing their growing, chaotic household of god-children.
