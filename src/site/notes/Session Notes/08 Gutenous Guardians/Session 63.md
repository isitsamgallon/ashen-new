# Session Summary 
After discovering that Chenris had covered Halfbie's room with additional Arithmancy and nonsense puzzles overnight, the party took Cas out to explore Blackwall. They visited a popular new bakery called Greg's, where they purchased prophetic bread (receiving cryptic fortunes like "Be careful of the third spoon" and "Beware of Denola"), then headed to the Ye Olde Pub Down The Road. There, they joined the Merchants' Guild to secure comfortable travel options toward the desert, with Halfbie attempting to negotiate closer trade ties between the Guild and the OFC government. The group also learned about a nuisance youth faction called "Better Blackwall" and a nightclub in a nearby cave, but both were closed for the day.

Upon returning home, Viktor completed a Hallowed spell designed to force Chenris to take control of Halfbie's body while Mordecai cast Zone of Truth. Chenris answered questions coldly and evasively, confirming his plan involves separating from Halfbie but refusing to share details, though he admitted he genuinely likes Drache and the Korrigan. When the spell ended, Halfbie awoke with complete amnesia, unable to recognize any party member or remember their months of shared adventures. As they tried to convince him to see a healer at the mage's guild, the party stepped outside to find the body of Denola, the name from Viktor's prophetic bread, lying dead in the town square.
