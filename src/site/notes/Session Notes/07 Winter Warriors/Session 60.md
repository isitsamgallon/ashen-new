# Session Summary 
As the party plans their raid on the Golden Fort, disagreement arises over whether to rescue the remaining New God eggs hidden around its perimeter. Mordecai argues that attempting a rescue would slow them down, risk exposure, and draw Auric’s attention, as releasing even one New God had already caused a massive energy surge. While Drache reluctantly agrees that the raid must take priority, Halfbie is deeply conflicted, torn between the children’s lives and the money the OFC desperately needs. After several plans fail, Halfbie proposes a daring solution: crafting three Bags of Holding to preserve the eggs’ heat and using hidden tunnels beneath the fort to retrieve them unseen. The party splits into teams, successfully infiltrates the tunnels, and retrieves the eggs. Along the way, Astra Goldmark intercepts them, not to stop the rescue, but to deliver a dire warning. Mordecai’s father has set something catastrophic in motion, and the party must not interfere with him, no matter what they see. Instead, Astra insists Auric must die, entrusting Drache with her dagger and extracting a promise that Thornwood will be left alone.

The party regroups as the raid begins, only for Robert Thornwood’s airships to arrive overhead, carrying hundreds of screaming hostages tied beneath them. Though enraged, the party is forced to accept the grim reality: Thornwood has technically upheld their agreement, and any delay will only worsen the hostages’ suffering. They push forward into the fort, where Astra confronts Mordecai over his impulsive attack on his father before leading them directly to Auric’s lair. In the dragon’s presence, Astra gives the signal, and Drache strikes her down without hesitation, fulfilling her own instructions and removing herself from the future she foresaw. As Astra falls and Auric rises from his hoard, the party forms ranks, fully committed to the battle ahead and the consequences that will follow.
