# Session Summary 
Exploring a locked R&D department, the party uncovered a dark laboratory filled with notes on failed experiments and a mysterious green liquid made from modified dragon blood. When Drache drank the liquid, he had a vision of a mountain valley filled with dragon eggs, guarded by a massive red dragon. Deeper in the facility, they discovered a cavern where two adult red dragons were held captive by floating needle-launchers, guarded by two invisible children. A tense standoff ensued where Halfbie was killed but revived, ultimately revealing the children—designated as experiments "3442" and "3443"—were powerful, sickly beings created from the blood of a god, who referred to themselves as "New Gods" (NG).

After naming the children Seer and Myst, the party convinced them to leave the cave and helped free the dragons. Using a magical artifact to rewrite a minor event, Halfbie summoned Xekai, who directed them to a golden dragon egg that Drache took to the mountain from his vision. There, time accelerated, and the egg hatched into a young girl named Cas, who possessed powerful psychic abilities and a humorous, megalomaniacal personality. With Cas joining Seer and Myst through a golden portal to safety, the party set their sights on rescuing the remaining "New Gods," acknowledging that their quest is far from simple.
