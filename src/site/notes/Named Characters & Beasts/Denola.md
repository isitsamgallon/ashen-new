### Overview
A test Procter for the [[Groups & Factions/Merchant Guild\|merchant guild]] that unfortunately tide in [[Locations/OFC/Blackwall/Blackwall\|Blackwall]]. she was notably mentioned in a Prophetic bread fortune from [[Groups & Factions/Greg's Bakery\|Greg's Bakery]]. She was meant to conduct the test for [[Other Information/Display Tags/The Party\|the party]] joining the guild but died before this could happen.
