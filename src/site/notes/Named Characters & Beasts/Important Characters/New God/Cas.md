### Overview
Cas is the third of the [[Other Information/Races/New Gods\|New Gods]] to be awaken. she is also blind. Cas looks like 5 year old girl with slight elven features. Cas seems to be some kind of [Chuunibyou](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ch%C5%ABniby%C5%8D) as the first thing she said was that she was Cas, Destroyer of worlds.
