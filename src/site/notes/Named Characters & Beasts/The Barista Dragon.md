### Overview
The dragons that run the Cafe in [[Locations/Strixhavem/Strixhaven\|Strixhaven]]'s teleport centre. They were first encountered in [[Session Notes/02 The War for The OFC's Freedom/Session 05\|Session 05]] when [[Other Information/Display Tags/The Party\|The Party]] were waiting for their gater to be ready, and [[The Party/Wurinn Fuuldrusch\|Wurinn Fuuldrusch]] wanted to get a drink. Unfortunately, the drink was sized and cost a lot more than what any of them had on hand. To pay the debt, [[The Party/Mordecai Reverence\|Mordecai Reverence]] traded their prized amulet for the coffee
